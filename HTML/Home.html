Coopersburg Inn

Welcome to coopersburg Inn. We are a family owned Inn in the outskirts of Coopersburg, and we would love for you to stop by. We have 15 bedrooms and 5 larger suites. We also serve breakfast lunch and dinner, which you could enjoy in our cafe or request it to your room. So if you are looking for a honeymoon retreat or just for a nice vacation location we have it all for you.
