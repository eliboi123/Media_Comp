Rooms

We currently have one location with 15 regular rooms and 5 suites. If you want to book a night at our Inn you need to call 202-555-0126 and they will help you get a Vacation booked.

The price of a Room is $140 a night but this price includes access to the rooms and all other services that we provide, including our pool, dining hall, game room, and our outdoor hiking paths.

Our suites are $300 a night and they have two bedrooms with two beds in each, a kitchen two bathrooms, and access to all of the mentioned services that we provide. The suite rooms are perfect if you are going on vacation as a family and you need more than one room, or if you just want a lot of space for yourself.
